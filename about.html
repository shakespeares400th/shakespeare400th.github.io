Who was William Shakespeare?

William Shakespeare, born on 23rd April 1564 and died on the 23rd April 1616 aged 52, was an English playwright, poet and actor who is considered by many the most influential writers for the English language. He was born and lived in Stratford-upon-Avon, Warwickshire and between 1585 and 1592, he moved to London and started his successful career in writing. Shakespeare is known for producing many comedies, histories, tragedies and romances. Most famous are plays such as Hamlet, Macbeth, Romeo and Juliet, Othello and many more.

In this website, I will discuss how influential Shakespeare was and consider what has Shakespeare ever done for us. I have split the reasons into 3 main parts: his language influences, his cultural/political influences and his social influences. This resource is one aiming to analyse and conclude at the implication of Shakespeare and how important Shakespeare is in modern day.
